## Privacy Policy for "Minimalist Calorie Counter" by Makstuff

**Minimalist Calorie Counter** is built with a "privacy-first" philosophy. All data gathered by the application stays on your device and is not transmitted to our servers or any third parties.

### Health Connect Integration

Our app offers optional integration with Google Health Connect to help you share your health data across the Android ecosystem.

**Data Access and Usage:**
If you choose to enable this integration, Minimalist Calorie Counter can write data related to **Nutrition** (energy and macronutrients) and **Body Weight** to Health Connect. The app can also read Health Connect data you authorize, including nutrition, weight, height, body fat, lean mass, and other readable Health Connect record types for the in-app meals view, goals defaults, cleanup tools, and CSV export.

**Purpose of Data Processing:**
*   **Data Export & Synchronization:** We share your recorded energy intake, nutrients, and body weight with Health Connect so that your progress is available to other health and fitness apps you trust, ensuring a unified view of your health journey.
*   **Health Connect Reading:** We read authorized Health Connect data locally to show meals, refresh goals, avoid duplicate imported meals, delete records you explicitly select, and export CSV files that you request.

**Privacy and Security Standards:**
*   **Limited Use Disclosure:** The use of information received from Health Connect will adhere to the [Health Connect Permissions Policy](https://support.google.com/googleplay/android-developer/answer/9888170), including the **Limited Use** requirements.
*   **No External Sharing:** We do not sell, rent, or share your health data with advertisers, data brokers, or any other third parties.
*   **Local Processing:** All interactions with health data are performed locally on your device. We do not store or process your health information on external servers. CSV exports are saved locally on your device when you request them.
